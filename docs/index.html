About

Art does not consist of only one interpretation, especially in the case of installation art. People can find ways to enjoy it from various directions. Taking advantage of this feature, I'm creating installation art with the following theme: Allowing the audience to submerge themselves in the place and face their own emotions. I plan the following work based on the feedback from the audience.
