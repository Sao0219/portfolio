Light and Space

Dimensions (WxDxH)：9" x 18" x 9" and 9" x 5" x 11"(H x W x D)
Materials： Styrofoam boad, Electrical tape, Cellophane, Artificial light



  • About Light and Space

    Through the space-based installation art I created in 2019, I learned that light and shadow have the ability to directly approach human emotions, whether it is to make artworks look powerful and awe-inspiring to the audience, or to create a sense of discomfort by creating an eerie atmosphere.

    Using black styrofoam boards and electrical tape, I created two boxes of different sizes. Through a small hole on the side of the box, I observed how light entering through a filter placed in the gap created by the height difference between the two boxes remodeled the uneven inner wall of the box made of white construction paper.
