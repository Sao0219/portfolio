#TinyArchitect Contest Entrees

Dimensions (WxDxH)：6in. x 3in. x 4in. / 16cm x 7cm x 11cm
Materials：Hands, Solder




Architect Sou Fujimoto's idea of "Architecture is Everywhere" led me to find architectural space in my hands. I felt more warmth from the human body than from any other materials. Even during the pandemic, I always wanted to put myself in a space where I could feel the connection with others. The warmth created by human hands has the power to heal others.

I won third place in the #TinyArchitect - Architecture is Everywhere contest was co-hosted by JAPAN HOUSE Los Angeles and Sou Fujimoto Architects! I'm extremely happy to be selected by Fujimoto-sensei. Also I received the video message from him about my submission, “Hand Pavilion”. His way of thinking process and perspective to my entry is very unique and interesting.
