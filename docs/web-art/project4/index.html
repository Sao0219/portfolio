  • About disequilibrium

    I added the element of "sound" to the web art using JavaScript. When the volume of the player's voice exceeds a certain level, the picture on the screen will change, and a sound reminiscent of anxiety will continue to play. With the addition of sound, I received feedback from the players that they were able to better deal with their emotions during the pandemic through the web space than with "Make a Coffee with the Servo Motor".
