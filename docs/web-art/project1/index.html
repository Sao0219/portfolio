No title: The Virtual Torii Gates (2020)

Materials：Rhinoceros, Blender



  • About No title: The Virtual Torii Gates

    This is the artwork that I submitted for ArtBash 2020.

    When people see installation art in a real space, they can visually come into contact with the work from 360 degrees. The goal here was to recreate the same visual experience by using virtual tools. I used GLTF files that allow a 3D model to be observed from any angles, even on the flat surface of a screen. People were able to see the art from any angles by moving the laptop cursor up, down, left, and right. Since the audience could only interact with the artwork through visual methods, the theme could not be fully achieved. After presenting this project, I wondered how to make it easier to access people’s emotions on a web space, where they cannot directly touch or smell the artwork.
