No title: Tsunagari (2020)

Dimensions (WxDxH)：4ft. x 4ft. x 6ft. / 1.2m x 1.2m x 1.8m
Materials：Red strings, Balloon, Wooden pieces


“Abstract forms stimulate people’s imagination.” I have been living with budgies since I was seven. Having lived with birds for most of my life, I can say that they are my closest companions that mitigate my worries and anxieties. When I left for college, I could no longer see my birds at home. However, it was the pigeons, birds that are abundant in American cities, that connected me to the city of Chicago and gave me comfort and peace in the new location.
