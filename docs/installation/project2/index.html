No title: The Torii Gates (2019)

Dimensions (WxDxH)：4ft. x 4ft. x 6ft. / 1.2m x 1.2m x 1.8m
Materials：Red strings, Balloon, Wooden pieces


“By using Five Senses to interact with the artwork, people find a Sense of Familiarity among the unfamiliar.” When one is exposed to or accepts the unknown, they may feel anxiety or discomfort. How do people embrace these feelings when they are put in such a situation? With this question in mind, I created a Japanese Shinto shrine in front of my college classmates. Most of them had no connections with shrines. My aim was to see how they would find a connection with something they have no intimacy with.
