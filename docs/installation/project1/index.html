No title: Death (2019)

Dimensions (WxDxH)：4ft. x 4ft. x 6ft. / 1.2m x 1.2m x 1.8m
Materials：Red strings, Balloon, Wooden pieces


“My work is to create ‘places’ that has meanings to the people, using ordinary ‘spaces’ ” One day I found a dead pigeon while walking on a street in Chicago. While I wanted to take care of the poor carcass, its body was tightly stuck on the street with blood and could not do anything for it. “No title: Death” is based on the frustration that day, and is a requiem for burying the pigeon in my heart. The boundary between life and death is impossible to cross tangibly. However, it can be connected emotionally by "mourning" the dead.
